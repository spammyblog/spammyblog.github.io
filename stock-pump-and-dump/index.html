QSMG: Stock pump and dump

Posted onApril 25, 2017 by Raz

Earlier this year we covered a flavour of spam which tried to convince the reader to buy out penny stocks promising huge gains within the coming weeks. Starting with the beginning of April, there was another huge wave of spam sent out in an attempt to get people to buy into a different stock.

As with the previous penny stocks emails, the people behind the spam buy massive amounts of shares of a poorly known company for dirt cheap. Then, when they’ve acquired a whole bunch of shares, they send out the spam in an attempt to get people to buy their shares at an artificially inflated price.
